    a word about us

    Mama Dak's Sisig is a school-based eatery that serves up a delicious variety of sizzling sisig to students, teachers, and staff. Known for our flavorful take on this classic Filipino dish, we focus on using fresh ingredients and creating meals that are both satisfying and affordable.

    Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a full meal, Mama Dak's Sisig is the place to be. We aim to bring a taste of home to the school community, offering comfort food that fills you up and brings joy with every bite.
